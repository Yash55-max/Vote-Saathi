📌 1. PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
1.1 Product Overview

CivicGuide AI is a context-aware election assistant designed to educate and guide citizens through the election process in an interactive, personalized, and accessible way.

1.2 Problem Statement
Low voter awareness
Confusing election procedures
Lack of localized information
First-time voters struggle
1.3 Target Users
Segment	Needs
First-time voters (18-22)	Step-by-step guidance
Rural users	Simple language + voice
Urban users	Fast info + comparison
Elderly	Accessibility + clarity
1.4 Goals
Simplify election participation
Provide personalized guidance
Improve voter awareness
Deliver real-time relevant info
1.5 Key Features
🔹 Smart Assistant
Context-aware responses
Personalized guidance
🔹 Voting Guide

System responds dynamically
